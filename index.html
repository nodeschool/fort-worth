Learn Node.js with a group in a casual environment!

NodeSchool Fort Worth is a monthly meetup to help people learn Node, JavaScript and other programming languages. Each meetup will focus on a workshopper from Node School. Our FTW mentors will be available to help through-out each class. Whether you're just getting started or have been developing for years, our meetups can help you learn something new and strengthen your knowledge!
